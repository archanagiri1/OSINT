# What is OSINT?

- OSINT stands for Open Source Intelligence
- It is the process of collecting information from publicly available sources
- Used by ethical hackers, investigators, journalists, and security researchers
- Sources include internet, social media, public records, news, forums

## Types of OSINT Sources
- Public websites
- Social media platforms
- Government records
- News articles
- Forums and dark web
- Satellite images
- Phone directories
